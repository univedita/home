Zendaya's History with Dance

Zendaya is also a dancer with clean and graceful moves. She has been dancing ever since she was 8 years old, starting with her being in a hip-hop troupe. In 2009 she auditioned to star in "Shake It Up" with Bella Thorne. The show was about two girls who got into a dance show. At the age of 19, Zendaya danced with Val on Dancing with the Stars.
